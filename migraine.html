A TCM reference for migraine / 少阳头痛

Side-headache is classically called 少阳经头痛 (Shaoyang-channel headache) — the temple-and-side region runs the Gallbladder and San-Jiao meridians. Treatment couples local points (止痛开关) with distal points (远端配穴), then refines by pattern (肝阳 / 风寒 / 痰湿 / 瘀血 / 气血虚) and side (left ~ liver ascent / right ~ stomach & lung descent).

偏头痛在中医多归为少阳经头痛（侧头部痛）——颞侧为胆经与三焦经循行之地。治则「经脉所过，主治所及」——局部取穴＋远端配穴，再按证型（肝阳/风寒/痰湿/瘀血/气血虚）与侧别（左偏肝升、右偏肺胃降）加减。

一、头部「止痛开关」Local pain-switch points

🎯 风池 GB20

Back of the head, in the depression between the SCM and trapezius muscles — about level with the earlobe. Disperses wind, clears the head, improves vertebrobasilar circulation. The #1 point in nearly every migraine protocol.

后脑勺下方，两条大筋（胸锁乳突肌与斜方肌）与枕骨之间凹陷处，大致与耳垂齐平。疏散风邪、清利头目、改善椎-基底动脉供血——几乎所有偏头痛方案之必选穴。

🎯 率谷 GB8

Directly above the ear tip, ≈ 1.5 cùn (2 finger-widths) into the hairline, on the temporal region. The classical 偏正头风 specific point — best for one-sided pulsating / vascular headache.

耳尖直上入发际约 1.5 寸（约两横指），侧头部。临床公认之偏头痛核心穴/特效穴——对搏动性痛、血管性头痛效果突出。

🎯 太阳穴 Ex-HN5

In the depression about one finger-width behind the midpoint of the line between eyebrow tip and outer canthus. The classical local "cooling" point — quick relief by light circular pressure during acute attack.

眉梢与目外眦连线中点，向后约一横指凹陷处。痛点所在区域——通络止痛快，发作期按揉常能较快「降温」。

🎯 百会 GV20

Meeting of all yang channels, at the top of the head (line from ear-tip to ear-tip ∩ midline). Lifts yang, clears the brain. Better for dull / qi-deficient headaches (worse with fatigue, accompanied by dizziness).

督脉「诸阳之会」。两耳尖连线与头顶正中线交点。升阳益气、醒脑安神——气血不足型（隐痛、劳累加重、伴头晕）更适合。

🎯 丝竹空 TE23 / 角孙 TE20

TE23 at the lateral end of the eyebrow; TE20 above the ear tip when ear is folded forward. Both lie on the headache radiation zone — classical line "丝竹空透率谷 treats one-sided headwind".

丝竹空＝眉梢外侧凹陷；角孙≈折耳向前时耳尖所指的发际处。都在头痛放射区——古传「丝竹空透率谷治偏正头风」。

⭐ Fastest acute-attack combo: 风池 + 率谷 + 太阳 — press to local soreness; see self-massage protocol below. ⭐ 发作期最快上手组合：风池＋率谷＋太阳，按到局部酸胀即可。

二、四肢「远端配穴」Distal points — root-level regulation

🌐 合谷 LI4

"面口合谷收" — opens the yangming qi-blood of the face / head, relieves distal pain, also calms nausea. (Avoid in pregnancy.)

「面口合谷收」——疏通头面阳明气血；远端镇痛，兼缓解恶心呕吐。妊娠忌针。

🌐 太冲 LR3

Migraine often involves Liver wind / Liver fire / emotional stress. LR3 calms ascending Liver yang, spreads Liver qi — cuts off the source of "qi rushing upward".

偏头痛多与肝风/肝火/情志相关——平肝潜阳、疏肝理气，从「气机上逆」的源头掐断。

🌐 外关 TE5 + 足临泣 GB41

Both open the Shaoyang channel — the very channel side-headache runs through. Up-down pair, frequently used when one-sided pain comes with tinnitus / dizziness.

偏头痛走少阳经——外关专通少阳，足临泣上下呼应，侧头痛伴耳鸣目眩时常配。

⭐ The classical "Open the Four Gates" (开四关) pairing: 合谷 + 太冲 (both sides) — one Yang + one Yin, one upper + one lower. Smoothes qi-blood and quells Liver wind; a workhorse combo for many practitioners. ⭐ 「开四关」经典配伍：合谷＋太冲（左右各一对）——一阳一阴、一上一下，理气活血、平肝熄风。

四、左右 ✕ 跳胀 — 取穴主次的微调Left vs right · pulsating vs distending

All side-headache walks the Shaoyang channel — but 左肝右肺: left tends to liver ascent, right to lung & stomach descent. Right-side migraine is more often paired with nausea / chest fullness / 苔厚腻 because the descent path is choked.

侧头痛皆走少阳——惟「左肝右肺、左升右降」：左痛偏肝（升发太过），右痛偏肺/胃（降不下去）。右侧偏头痛更常伴恶心反胃、胸闷、苔厚腻，痛势带「闷堵感」。

⭐ Right-side rule of thumb: if pressing the head makes the pain worse, "the fire is on top, qi is reversed" — work the feet (太冲, 足三里) first, head last. ⭐ 右侧诀窍：若按头反而更蹦更烦，说明「火在上、气逆」——少按头上、多按脚下（太冲、足三里），让气降下来才是正道。

五、发作期自我按揉（安全速效版）Self-massage during an attack — safe quick-relief

For temporary relief — NOT a substitute for medical care. Press to bearable soreness/distension, 2–3 min per point. Don't dig hard at the head if it makes the throb worse — work the feet first.

仅作临时缓解，不能替代就医。按揉力度以酸胀可耐受为度，每穴 2-3 分钟。头上按反而更顶者，先按脚下。

  1. ① 太冲 (both sides) — steady deep pressure with the thumb, exhale as you press. 2-3 min per side. Settles the upward-rushing qi.
  2. ① 太冲（双侧）——拇指沉稳压压，呼气时加力，每侧 2-3 min。先把「从下往上顶」的势头摁住。
  3. ② 内关 (if any nausea / chest-fullness) — 1-2 min on the affected side. Descends stomach qi.
  4. ② 内关（若有恶心或胸闷）——患侧 1-2 min。降胃气、宽胸膈。
  5. ③ 合谷 — opposite-side thumb pinching the tiger's mouth peak; sustain pressure. (Avoid in pregnancy.) 1-2 min.
  6. ③ 合谷——对侧拇指按压虎口最高酸胀处，保持持续压力。孕妇禁用。1-2 min。
  7. ④ Head last — gentle circular presses on 风池 (push toward the opposite jaw, NOT straight back), 率谷, 太阳. Close eyes, slow exhale.
  8. ④ 头部最后——轻揉风池（朝对侧下颌方向推，不要往后硬顶）、率谷、太阳穴。配合闭眼深呼吸。

六、侧头痛 ≠ 偏头痛Side-headache is NOT always migraine

"Side-headache" describes location; "migraine" is a specific diagnosis (ICHD criteria). The TCM "少阳头痛" framework works on the channel/syndrome level — it doesn't replace a neurology workup.

「侧头痛」是描述位置，「偏头痛」（Migraine）是具体诊断。中医的「少阳头痛」是按经络/证候归类——不能取代神经科评估。

Migraine (simplified ICHD): 4–72 h; one-sided OR bilateral; pulsating; moderate-severe; worsens with walking / bending / head shaking; AND ≥1 of {nausea/vomiting · photophobia AND phonophobia together}.

偏头痛（ICHD 简化）：持续 4-72 小时；单侧或双侧；搏动/跳痛；中-重度；走路/弯腰/晃头加重；伴 ≥1 项 {恶心/呕吐 · 怕光 AND 怕吵}。

Other possibilities for side-headache: tension headache (band-like), cluster headache (vicious, orbital, with tearing), trigeminal neuralgia (electric shock-like, trigger spots), cervicogenic headache (referred from upper-cervical or jaw muscles), temporal arteritis (≥50yr, scalp tender, blood-sedimentation up), intracranial cause (need imaging).

侧头痛其他来源：紧张型头痛（像箍紧）、丛集性头痛（眼眶剧痛 + 流泪鼻塞）、三叉神经痛（电击样 + 触发点）、颈源性头痛（颈/咬肌牵涉）、颞动脉炎（≥50 岁 + 咀嚼累 + 视力变化）、颅内占位/感染等需影像排查。

🚨 Seek urgent neuro / emergency care if:
  • First-ever sudden severe attack ("worst headache of my life")
  • Vision loss, doubled vision, droopy face, slurred speech, limb weakness, numbness
  • Fever AND stiff neck OR altered consciousness
  • ≥50 yr first-time onset, especially with chewing fatigue or vision changes (temporal arteritis)
  • Pregnancy / postpartum sudden severe headache
  • Pattern change: previously occasional → now daily and escalating; analgesics no longer work
🚨 出现下列情况须立即就医排查器质性问题：
  • 首次极其剧烈（「人生最痛的一次」）
  • 视力突然缺损/重影、口角歪、肢体麻木无力、说话含糊
  • 高热 + 颈硬 或 意识改变
  • ≥50 岁新发，伴咀嚼累 + 视力下降（考虑颞动脉炎）
  • 怀孕/产后突发严重头痛
  • 痛模式改变：原偶发 → 现天天痛、加重、止痛药不管用

七、为什么按 率谷 / 角孙 常常立刻舒缓？Why pressing 率谷 / 角孙 often gives rapid relief

Both points sit directly over the temporalis muscle attachment + superficial temporal artery / temporal-fascia tension zone. They share the Shaoyang channel territory where side-headache concentrates. Pressure briefly relaxes the local tension knot AND modulates the vascular pulsation through reflex pathways.

两穴正压在颞肌附着、颞浅动脉走行、颞筋膜张力集中点。少阳经气在此「卡住」——按对点等于短暂把这团张力松开，并通过反射弧调节血管搏动。

What this tells you: if pressing 率谷 / 角孙 reliably calms the headache, you're most likely on the 「Shaoyang temporalis-tension」 spectrum — either a vascular-tension type of migraine OR a temporal-fascia / cervicogenic side-headache. Both respond well to gentle sustained pressure (not rubbing) coordinated with slow exhale.

这告诉你：按率谷 / 角孙能明显缓解，多属少阳颞肌张力型——血管张力型偏头痛，或颞筋膜/颈源性侧头痛。两者皆宜持续稳压（非搓揉）＋慢呼气配合。
