About this site

This is an educational tool for exploring 子午流注 (zǐ wǔ liú zhù) — the Traditional Chinese Medicine framework that maps the flow of qi through the twelve organ meridians across a single day. The clock at the left of the screen tracks your local time and highlights the meridian currently at its peak.

本站为学习子午流注之教育性工具——传统中医所立之框架，述气循十二脏腑经络，于昼夜之间运行不息。屏幕左侧之时辰钟跟随本地时间，标示当令之经脉。

来源Sources

The cosmological dial design — the multi-ring layout combining 24-hour numbers, 24 directional terms (二十四方位), 12 Earthly Branches (地支), 12 zodiac animals (生肖), and the 12 organ sectors with their five-tone musical correspondences (五音) — is inspired by KunVinn/CTM-Clock.

本钟之多环排列——合二十四小时刻度、二十四方位、十二地支、十二生肖、十二脏腑分区与五音之配——取法于 KunVinn/CTM-Clock。

The Knowledge Graph section follows the entity taxonomy of TCMM, a research-grade TCM knowledge graph integrating six databases (CPMCP, TCMBANK, SymMap, TCMID, PharMeBINet, PrimeKG) into a unified resource of over three million records. Their published categories — Property (性), Flavour (味), Tropism (归经), Medicinal Material, Prescription, Syndrome (证候), TCM Symptom, Disease, Ingredient, Target/Gene, Pathway, and others — form the structural backbone of this site's knowledge browser.

「知识图谱」一节循 TCMM 之实体分类——其为学术级中医知识图谱，整合六大数据库（CPMCP、TCMBANK、SymMap、TCMID、PharMeBINet、PrimeKG）逾三百万条记录于一体。其所定之类别——性、味、归经、本草、方剂、证候、症状、病、成分、靶点、通路等——即为本站知识浏览器之骨架。

五音The Five-Tone Music System

Each organ in TCM is associated with one of the five pentatonic tones (角、徵、宫、商、羽). Specific traditional Chinese pieces — guqin classics, ancient melodies, and folk songs — are believed to harmonise the corresponding meridian. Track names listed for each hour are drawn from the CTM-Clock playlist; you can find recordings on most music streaming services.

中医谓五脏各配一音（角、徵、宫、商、羽）。古琴名曲、古典曲目与民间小调皆有调和某经之效。各时辰所列曲名取自 CTM-Clock 之曲目表，于多数音乐平台均可寻得录音。

使用How to use it

The clock updates in real time. As each two-hour window arrives, the Today page automatically refreshes to show that meridian's full guidance — what's happening, practices that nourish, what to avoid, supportive herbs, and the recommended music.

钟以实时更新。每入新时辰，「此刻」页将自动刷新，呈该经之完整指引——此时之机、所宜之事、所忌之事、补益之本草、与所配之音乐。

Browse Twelve Hours for a complete overview, study the Five Elements with their generation and control cycles, explore the Knowledge Graph for deeper TCM concepts, and use Daily Practices to commit to one small habit per hour-window.

「十二时辰」可总览全日；「五行」可研相生相克；「知识图谱」可探深层中医概念；「日修」可承诺每时辰行一小修。

技术Site structure

This is a static site built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no build step, no dependencies. Each of the six pages shares the same sidebar (clock + now-strip) and the same data file (assets/data.js), so adding new content is a matter of editing one place. Drop the entire folder onto any web host (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, even a USB stick) and it works.

本站为静态网页，仅以 HTML、CSS 与 JavaScript 写就——无须打包，无外部依赖。各页共享同一侧栏（时辰钟与当前条）与同一数据文件（assets/data.js），故新增内容只需于一处编辑。整目录可直接置于任一网页空间（GitHub Pages、Netlify、Cloudflare Pages 乃至 U 盘）即可运行。
