Where your data goes

This is a static educational site. By default, almost everything stays on your device. The two exceptions are clearly opt-in: AI tongue analysis (sends to Google) and emailing a report to a practitioner (relays through Resend). Below is the full picture.

本站为静态之教育网页。常态之下，所有数据皆留于您之设备。有二例须主动同意者：舌诊之 AI 分析（送至 Google）与转送报告之电邮（经 Resend 中继）。详情如下。

留于设备 Stays on your device local only

Tongue photos by default, your zoom and language preferences, music-player state (current track and play position), saved practitioner contact, and your in-page tongue-finding notes. All held in your browser's localStorage; never sent anywhere unless you press a button that says it will.

舌照（常态）、缩放与语言之偏好、音乐播放之状态（曲目与时点）、所记之医师联系、舌象自察之笔记，皆存于本机之 localStorage，非按明示之钮则不外送。

送 Google（须同意） Goes to Google — only if you opt in opt-in

On the Tongue Diagnosis page you may choose to run AI analysis. That sends your photo and a fixed prompt to Google's Gemini API using your own free key. We never see the photo or the result. Google's terms apply; their stated policy is that free-tier prompts may be used to improve their models. Don't opt in if that's not acceptable.

于舌诊页可自择 AI 分析。其将舌照与既定之提示送至 Google Gemini API，用您自备之免费密钥。本站不见其照、不见其果。Google 之条款适用；其声明：免费档之提示可能用于改进其模型。若不能接受，请勿启用此功能。

送医师（经 Resend 中继） Emails to a practitioner — only when you press Send opt-in

If you fill in the report form and press Send, your name, email, the practitioner's email, your note, the report HTML, and the photo are POSTed to a serverless function that relays the message through Resend. The function is stateless — it stores nothing. Resend processes the email in transit per their policy. A copy is CC'd back to you so you can see exactly what was sent.

若填妥报告并按送出，则姓名、邮箱、医师邮箱、附言、报告 HTML、舌照随之 POST 至无服务器函数，经 Resend 中继。函数不留任何记录；Resend 仅作传送中之处理，按其政策。报告同时副本回送至您之邮箱，所发何物，您皆可见。

医师名录 About listed practitioners public

The Practitioners directory only lists people who returned a signed consent form permitting publication of: name, credentials, location, languages, specialties, optional photo and website, and one contact email. Any practitioner can request removal at any time.

「医师」名录仅刊载已签同意书者所允刊载之内容：姓名、资格、所在地、语言、专长、可选之照片与诊所网址、一组联系邮箱。任何医师皆可随时请求撤销。

无追踪 · No analytics, no third-party tracking

The site does not load analytics scripts, advertising tags, or social-media pixels. There are no cookies set by this site beyond what your browser itself uses for HTTP. The only network traffic beyond loading the page is to fonts (system fonts only — no Google Fonts), to Google Gemini if you opt in to AI analysis, and to the email-relay function if you press Send.

本站不载任何分析脚本、广告标签、社交平台之追踪像素，亦不设本站之 Cookie（除浏览器 HTTP 自用者外）。除载页所需者，对外之流量仅有三端：字体（皆为系统字体，不引 Google Fonts）；若启用 AI 分析则至 Google Gemini；按送出钮则至电邮中继函数。

查询与撤销 · Asking us, or asking us to forget

If you'd like to know what's on our side (very little — see above), have a sent report scrubbed from Resend's logs, or ask a practitioner listing be taken down, write to aikaikong@gmail.com. We'll respond within 7 days.

欲知本站留有何物（如上所述，极少）、欲请 Resend 删去送出之报告、或欲撤销医师之刊载，请致信 aikaikong@gmail.com，七日内回复。
