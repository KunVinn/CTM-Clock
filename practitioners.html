If you are a credentialed TCM practitioner and would like to be listed here so users can send you their self-check reports directly, please email the site owner at the address below with:

  • Your full name (and optionally Chinese name)
  • Your professional credentials and the jurisdiction(s) where you are licensed
  • Languages you practice in
  • Your practice location
  • A short bio (~80 words) — feel free to include both English and Chinese
  • The email address users should send their report to
  • Optionally: a clinic website and a professional photo

We will reply with a short consent form (PRACTITIONER_CONSENT.md) that explains exactly what will be published and how to withdraw at any time. Once you've returned the signed consent, your listing usually appears within 7 days.

Listing is free. We do not accept payment in exchange for a listing — this is an editorial directory of practitioners we personally vouch for, not an advertising platform.
