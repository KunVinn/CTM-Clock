Wǔ Xíng · The Five Phases

Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water

In TCM, every organ, season, taste, emotion, and color belongs to one of five interrelated phases. They flow into each other (生 generation) and balance each other (克 control).

在中医之中，凡脏腑、季节、五味、五志、五色，皆归属于五行之一。彼此相依而生（相生），相制而平（相克）。
